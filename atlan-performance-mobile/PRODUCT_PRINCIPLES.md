# Product Principles — Atlan Performance

These are hard constraints. Every screen, string, model field, and test must honor them.

1. **Life interruption is the default state, not the exception.**
2. **The app absorbs disruption silently instead of marking it red.**
3. **Scientific depth lives one tap below the surface.** Science appears behind contextual "Why" affordances, never on display by default.
4. **The dashboard answers only two questions:**
   - What should I do today?
   - Is the week still on track?
5. **No streak pressure.**
6. **No leaderboard pressure.**
7. **No red missed-session shame states.**
8. **No "you are behind" language.**
9. **Spanish is not a translation layer; it is a first-class product language.** Selected before account creation, equal visual priority, wraps instead of shrinking.
10. **Wet Mode must work offline and be operable with wet hands.** Large touch zones, directional gestures, no precision-tap dependency.

## Forbidden everywhere (UI, copy, telemetry, model fields)
streaks · leaderboards · peer ranking · flames · trophies · badges · notification-badge pressure · "missed" · "streak broken" · "behind plan" · red failure states for training disruption · "good athlete / bad athlete" framing · motivational hype inside science content.

## Color discipline tied to principles
- **Coral** is rare — only productive action, completion, and high-signal accents. Never for errors, guilt, or missed sessions.
- **Tide** marks science, calm progress, selected state, and Why affordances.
- Disruption (swap / skip) is never failure and never red.
